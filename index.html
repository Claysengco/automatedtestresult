NNHS(Navotas National High School)

Navotas National High School was established on September 7, 2004, making it a relatively new institution compared to other schools in the Philippines. Since its establishment, it has aimed to provide quality education to the students of Navotas City and help them develop academically, socially, and morally.

Why NNHS is good? (NNHS)

NNHS is equipped with modern facilities and resources to support effective teaching and learning. It has well-equipped classrooms, science and computer laboratories, a library, and sports facilities that cater to various interests and talents of the students.

Computer Laboratory (NNHS)

The computer laboratory at Navotas National High School is a dedicated space equipped with computers and other necessary equipment to facilitate the teaching and learning of computer-related subjects. It serves as a hub for students to enhance their digital literacy skills, learn programming, conduct research, and access educational resources.

Complex (NNHS)

Navotas National High School Complex is a dynamic and inclusive learning environment designed to cater to the diverse needs of students. The complex provides a range of facilities and resources to enhance the overall educational experience.
